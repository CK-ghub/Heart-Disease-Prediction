# Heart-Disease-Prediction
Classification models to predict 10-year risk of future coronary heart disease (CHD)

# About the dataset:
The dataset is publically available on the Kaggle website, and it is from an ongoing cardiovascular study on residents of the town of Framingham, Massachusetts. The classification goal is to predict whether the patient has 10-year risk of future coronary heart disease (CHD).The dataset provides the patientsâ€™ information. It includes over 4,240 records and 15 attributes.

Source: https://www.kaggle.com/amanajmera1/framingham-heart-study-dataset

# Objective
Objective: To build a classification model that predicts heart disease in a subject. (note the target column to predict is 'TenYearCHD' where CHD = Coronary heart disease)
